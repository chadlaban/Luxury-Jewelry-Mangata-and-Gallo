About Mangata and Gallo

Mangata and Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in special occasions like engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. The jewelry company primarily operates online, with a small storefront in Austin, Texas that includes an atelier for browsing. Mangata & Gallo's selection is known for its high-quality craftsmanship and classic, timeless designs.

Mariana, the owner and lead designer, is an Austin local who is highly regarded for her unique jewelry designs. After graduating from design school with a specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing, Mariana opened the first Mangata & Gallo store in her hometown. She grew her business online, expanding her reach to clients looking for exceptional jewelry for their special moments.

Over the years, Mariana has managed every aspect of the business, from designing jewelry to handling marketing and running the e-commerce platform.
